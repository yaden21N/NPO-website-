Home Page

At Somerset West Night Shelter, our objectives are deeply rooted in our commitment to supporting the homeless community. We aim to provide a safe and secure environment where individuals in need can find refuge. Our primary goal is to offer immediate relief through shelter, food, and basic necessities, ensuring that no one has to endure the hardships of homelessness alone.

Despite the difficulties, homelessness is not always a permanent state, and with adequate support systems, including affordable housing, job opportunities, and mental health services, many individuals can transition back into stable living environments. Addressing homelessness requires a collective effort from governments, communities, and social organizations.

Community Impact :

Over the years, the Somerset West Night Shelter has made a significant impact on reducing homelessness in the Helderberg District. By addressing both immediate needs and long-term rehabilitation, the shelter helps individuals regain stability and reintegrate into society. Their efforts also foster greater community awareness and involvement in tackling homelessness.

When someone arrives at the shelter, our dedicated volunteers offer the homeless secure accommodation, along with an evening meal, breakfast, and access to warm showers. Beyond immediate assistance, we strive to empower our residents by connecting them with essential services such as healthcare, counseling, and job training. We believe in fostering a sense of dignity and hope, helping individuals rebuild their lives and regain their independence. Additionally, we conduct various workshops among many other services.

We are also dedicated to raising awareness about homelessness in our community, advocating for long-term solutions, and collaborating with local organizations and volunteers to create a supportive network. Our ultimate objective is to not only alleviate the effects of homelessness but to address its root causes, working towards a future where everyone has the opportunity to live with security and dignity. If many people make small changes , together we can make a big difference by taking many homeless people off the street. We still believe that humanity is alive !
